Join us!

There are many ways to get involved.

Join working groups

CASS has working groups on many different topics.

Contact the chair of working groups of interest. Or suggest a new working group to the CASS Steering Committee.

Connect with CASS members

If you're interested in specific areas of the software ecosystem, there may be a CASS member organization actively engaged with that community.

Reach out to the leadership of the member organization.

Become a member of CASS

We welcome projects and organizations with similar scientific software stewardship missions to consider joining CASS as a member. DOE sponsorship is not required.

Contact the CASS Steering Committee.
