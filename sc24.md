## CASS Events at [SC24](https://sc24.supercomputing.org){: .text-pink-500 underline}
### November 17-22, 2024

Member organizations of the Consortium for the Advancement of Scientific Software (CASS) and projects they support are organizing a number of event at the [SC24 conference](https://sc24.supercomputing.org){: .text-pink-500 underline}.  Please join us in Atlanta or online!
