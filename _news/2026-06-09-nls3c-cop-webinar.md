last_modified_at: 2026-07-20

The June webinar in the [National Laboratories Sustainable Scientific Software Conference](https://s3c.sandia.gov/)'s (NLS³C's) Community of Practice (CoP) webinar series, on "Like-Minded Organizations" featured short presentations about CASS, and about the Better Scientific Software ([BSSw.io](https://bssw.io)) resource site, supported by member organization PESO, in addition to presentations about the INnovative Training Enabled by a Research Software Engineer Community of Trainers ([INTERSECT](https://intersect-training.org/)), the Research Software Alliance ([ReSA](https://www.researchsoft.org/)), and the US Research Software Engineer Association ([US-RSE](https://us-rse.org/)).
