The User/Developer Experience (UDX) Working group hosts a regular discussion hour on UDX topics to complement our every-other-month webinars. 

This month, we will discuss strategies for doing user experience (UX) research and UX work. Most teams don’t do anything formal, but generally have some means of gathering feedback or assessing user experience. We’ll talk casually about what we’ve tried, what’s worked and what didn’t, and ways we’d like to level up.
