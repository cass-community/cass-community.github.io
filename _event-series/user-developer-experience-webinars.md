User/Developer Experience Webinars feature speakers who highlight best practices and challenges for usable scientific software.

## Who should attend
Webinars are targeted toward scientific and HPC software developers as well as UX researchers. No user experience expertise is needed. Join us as we learn together.

## Schedule and format
Webinars occur on the first Tuesday of odd months at 12:00pm - 1:00pm in the US Eastern time zone. Audience questions and discussion will be encouraged. Add the [webinar events](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0?cid=Y181YjFjNzU0MDE5MmU5ZTY2OTE1MDlkMGFjMWQzOWIzNmY3ZGIxNDNlNzRhMTBhMTRhOWEzY2Q3MDVmMTI1NzhmQGdyb3VwLmNhbGVuZGFyLmdvb2dsZS5jb20) to your calendar.

## Registration
